TruScore Grading (TSG)

2. The Problem

     • Professional grading is expensive, slow, and opaque.
     • Collectors waste money submitting cards that aren’t worth grading.
     • Human graders = inconsistent, biased, non-transparent.
     • PSA’s acquisition of SGC & Beckett risks monopolizing grading standards.




3. The Solution

TruScore Grading (TSG) – An AI-driven pre-grading app that: - Analyzes card condition using smartphone
scans - Delivers objective, explainable grade estimates - Highlights defects: corners, edges, surface,
centering - Shows whether grading is financially worthwhile, using real-time market data




4. Why Now

     • Card market = multi-billion dollar ecosystem
     • Grading volumes are booming — but so is frustration
     • No scalable, AI-first consumer tool exists
     • Consolidation is increasing control and bias in grading
     • Collectors demand transparency, consistency, and control




5. Target Market

     • Hobbyists, flippers, and investors submitting cards frequently
     • Millions of collectors worldwide
     • Heavy repeat behavior: multiple scans per week/month
